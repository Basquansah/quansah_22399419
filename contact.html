Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take? ▼

Standard delivery takes 2–5 business days. Same-day delivery is available in Accra, Kumasi, and Tamale for orders placed before 2 PM. Prime members enjoy free next-day delivery on millions of items.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy on most items. Items must be in original condition. Electronics have a 15-day return window. To start a return, visit your Order History and click "Return or Replace Items."

How do I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email once your order is dispatched. You can also track orders in real-time under "Returns & Orders" in your account.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money), Visa, MasterCard, bank transfers, and cash on delivery in select areas.

How do I sell on ShopNow?

Registering as a seller is free! Visit our Seller Centre, create an account, list your products, and start selling to our 500,000+ customers. We handle payments, and you get paid every two weeks.
