Our Mission

ShopNow was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to make quality products accessible to every Ghanaian household at fair prices, with fast and reliable delivery.

Inspired by global e-commerce leaders like Amazon, we believe that every customer deserves a seamless shopping experience — from browsing to doorstep delivery.

We are committed to empowering local sellers, creating jobs, and building a sustainable digital economy across West Africa.

Our Journey

2020

ShopNow Founded

Launched in Accra with just 200 products and a team of 5. Our first 100 orders were delivered by bicycle in Osu.

2021

Expanded to Kumasi & Takoradi

Opened two new fulfilment centres and onboarded 5,000 sellers across Ghana. Launched same-day delivery in Accra.

2022

Launched Mobile App & Prime Service

Our Android and iOS app hit 100,000 downloads in the first month. Introduced ShopNow Prime for free 1-day delivery.

2023

500,000 Customers Milestone

Reached half a million registered customers and expanded our electronics and fashion categories significantly.

2025–26

West Africa Expansion & AI Features

Launched in Nigeria and Côte d'Ivoire. Introduced AI-powered product recommendations and voice search.
